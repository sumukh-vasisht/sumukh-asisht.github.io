Who am I?

Professional Introduction

Hi I'm Sumukh Vasisht Shankar an aspiring Data Scientist and Tech Enthusiast. Am currently pusuing Masters in Data Science at Northeastern University, Boston. Over the course of my Bachelor’s degree in Information Science, I understood that data tells us more than just numbers - it helps us in so many things - from understanding the business by itself, the users of a business and their desires to the decisions a bot should make based on it’s historical data - the uses of data are simply vast. And my interest to explore more in this field propelled me to do a master’s degree in Data Science.

Prior to enrolling in this program, during my undergrad, I started off as a software intern where I was designing websites and apps for clients. While I enjoyed interacting with clients and building the projects according to their needs, I realized that I was drawn to working with data and the mathematics behind it. I eventually migrated to the field of data science. I am now skilled in Python, R, SQL, Data Visualization tools and techniques, Machine Learning algorithms and frameworks, databases like MySQL, Google Firebase and MongoDB, big data frameworks like Spark and Hadoop.

After working in quite a few professional roles, I decided that I want to be in a data scientist role where I will be able to focus on the data of the company & it’s users and contribute to understanding it and providing meaningful insights that’ll lead to the company improving it’s business. This is why I am seeking an internship or a co-op in Data Science where I will be able to apply my technical skills with the knowledge of data science I am learning in my program.
