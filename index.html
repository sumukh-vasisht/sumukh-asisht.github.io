Sumukh Vasisht Shankar

I am Sumukh Vasisht Shankar, a dedicated Data Scientist with a passion for leveraging advanced machine learning and natural language processing techniques to solve complex problems in cardiovascular data science. With a strong foundation in both engineering and data science, I have had the privilege of working on cutting-edge research projects at Yale University's Cardiovascular Data Science Lab advised by Dr. Rohan Khera and Northeastern University's Geometric Learning Lab advised by Dr. Robin Walters. My work spans a diverse range of topics, from novel methods and applications of Deep Learning to Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) methods.

My research interests span from development of new deep learning models to applications of deep learning to a diverse set of applications in science and engineering.
